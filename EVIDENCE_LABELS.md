# Evidence Status Labels

## VALIDATED
Reproduced ≥2 times by independent runs. Confidence cap: 0.9.

## PARTIAL
Reproduced once or has known limitations. Confidence cap: 0.7.

## BELOW-VALIDATED-RANGE
Result is honest limitation, not failure. System operates reliably at higher parameters. Confidence cap: 0.3.

## UNRELIABLE_ALIGNMENT
Registration failed — result is inconclusive, not negative.

## BAD_SCAN
Input data quality insufficient for analysis.

## SPECULATIVE
Not yet tested. Confidence cap: 0.3.
